# Code of conduct

We want aikit collaboration to be safe, direct, and useful.

## Expected behavior

- Treat people with respect, including when reviewing mistakes or disagreeing on design.
- Critique code and evidence, not a person's identity, experience, or motives.
- Welcome questions and explain project-specific context without ridicule.
- Protect private information and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope and safety, while remaining free to disagree
  constructively.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, sustained disruption,
or retaliation are not tolerated in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

The public conduct contact is **TBD before release** because this checkout has no verified remote.
Until one is published, ask the project owner through the private channel by which you received
the source for a confidential reporting route. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive
personal information. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors
when needed to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical,
with conflicts of interest recused.

This policy applies to repository discussions, reviews, issue trackers, community chat, events,
and public representation of the project.
